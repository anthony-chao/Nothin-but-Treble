Welcome to Nothin' but Treble!

Nothin' but Treble is an interactive music sheet where you can drag and drop quarter notes onto a music sheet, and play said notes. Before we get started, if you do not have a music background, please watch this video to familarize yourself with the basics of writing music!

2 / 3

Pick up a note and place it on the music sheet!

Grab one of the quarter notes and place it anywhere on the music sheet. You can only place one note onto a beat, which is each column, and you can place up to 12 notes onto this music sheet.

3 / 3

Pick your instrument and play!

You can choose between a clarinet, trumpet or saxophone to play the notes that you put on the music sheet. Click the play button to hear your masterpiece! When you want to start over, click the reset button and give it another go!
