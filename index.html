Welcome to Nothin' but Treble!

Some background on music sheets - you can place music notes onto the sheet and notes will be played left to right. This music sheet is in a 4/4 time signature which means there are four beats in one bar. It is also in treble clef, which is used for higher sounding notes.

So let's start off grabbing by a Quarter Note (which is 1 beat) and place it on the Music Sheet. You can only place one note on each beat. Once you're satisfied with your notes, select an instrument and click Play. Name your masterpiece after you're done!
